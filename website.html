The LAYERLAB Spectrum

LAYERLAB exists at the intersection of food science and color theory. Brand Story

Built in Layers. Informed by Experiments.

LAYERLAB is an AI-first snack company exploring what happens when you treat flavor, color, and nutrition like a series of controlled experiments. Every bar is an exercise in precise layering: taste, texture, macros, and visuals are all calibrated for modern consumers who expect more from their snacks. The metallic, aluminum-inspired packaging reflects a lab environment; the bold colors showcase the natural hero ingredients inside.

Sustainability & Packaging

Thoughtful Materials, Future-Ready Design

Our wrappers use aluminum-accented, high-barrier films designed to protect color-sensitive ingredients like matcha and spirulina while minimizing material waste. We prioritize recyclable and mono-material options where possible and design our cartons to ship efficiently in compact cases—less air, less impact.
